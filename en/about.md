# About JenaLab

JenaLab is a creative tech startup building an integrated creative ecosystem for creators and startups. We support people's journeys of "Record, Grow, and Share," creating a world where anyone can bring their ideas to life and continuously expand the value of creation.

> Human narrative is data.
> Data has a story.
> Stories connect people.

---

## Philosophy

- **Capture** — Transform human narrative into structured data.
- **Tell** — Generate and visualize stories with AI.
- **Connect** — Stories create real relationships.

---

## Our Mission

- **Record** — Build tools that record the creative process.
- **Grow** — Provide an environment for growth through education and mentoring.
- **Share** — Connect completed works and ideas with the world.

---

## The Narrative Stack

- **Contexta** — Capture human narrative as structured knowledge graphs. *(Coming Soon)*
- **Storida** — Turn narratives into stories and picture books with AI.
- **Armo** — Stories connect people to people.

---

## Open Source Philosophy

We build what we need, then open it to the world. JenaLab builds tools internally and releases them under the MIT open source license.

---

## Why We Exist

Anyone can be a creator now. Yet the tools to record, paths to grow, and stages to share remain fragmented.

JenaLab bridges these gaps — building a world where learning, making, and sharing flow as one continuous journey.

---

## Our Promise

- **Human-Centered Technology** — We develop technology that amplifies human creativity.
- **Sustainable Growth** — We build a sustainable creative ecosystem where art and technology coexist.
- **Community First** — We build a community that grows together, not alone.
